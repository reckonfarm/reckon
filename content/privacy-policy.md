# Dryline — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 5/30/2026
**Last updated:** 5/30/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dryline ("Dryline," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use dryline.farm and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this policy.


## 1. Who we are
The Service is operated by Dryline, LLC, located at Winnett, MT. You can reach us at dryline@gmail.com.

## 2. Information we collect

**Information you give us:**
- **Account information** — your email address and a password (or sign-in via a one-time email code or Google). Authentication is handled through Supabase.
- **Profile and operation details** you choose to enter — for example, your county, location, or ranch/operation information.
- **Marketplace content** — hay listings you post, including descriptions, prices, quantities, and contact preferences.
- **Communications** — messages you send through the Service and feedback you submit through our in-app feedback widget.
- **Phone number** — only if you opt in to text (SMS) alerts.

**Information collected automatically:**
- **Usage and device data** — pages viewed, features used, approximate location derived from IP, browser/device type, and similar analytics, collected to understand and improve the Service.
- **Cookies and similar technologies** — used to keep you signed in and to measure usage. See Section 6.

We do **not** collect government identifiers (such as Social Security or FSA farm numbers), bank or payment-card numbers, or sensitive financial account information through the Service.

## 3. How we use information
We use information to:
- create and maintain your account and keep you signed in;
- provide the Service's features — drought information, LFP eligibility indicators and estimates, and the hay marketplace;
- send you transactional messages (e.g., sign-in codes, account notices) and, if you opt in, alerts you request;
- operate, secure, debug, and improve the Service using analytics and your feedback;
- communicate with you about updates, and (with your consent where required) about new features;
- comply with law and enforce our Terms of Service.

## 4. How we share information
We do not sell your personal information. We share it only:
- **With service providers** who process data on our behalf, including: Supabase (authentication and database), Vercel (hosting), Resend (email delivery), our analytics provider (usage analytics), and Twilio (SMS, if you opt in to text alerts). These providers may process your information only to provide services to us.
- **Between marketplace users** — information in a listing (and the contact method you choose) is visible to other users so a transaction can happen. Don't put anything in a listing you don't want shared.
- **For legal reasons** — to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, or protect the rights, safety, and property of Dryline, our users, or others.
- **In a business transfer** — if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy.

## 5. Data from public sources
The Service displays data from public sources such as the U.S. Drought Monitor and NOAA. That data is about geographic areas, not about you, and is governed by those sources' own terms.

## 6. Cookies
We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in (session cookies) and to measure usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect sign-in and some features.

## 7. Data retention
We keep account information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data (see Section 9). We may retain limited information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security logs).

## 8. Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your choices and rights
- **Access / update** — you can view and update your account information in the Service.
- **Deletion** — you can request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting dryline@gmail.com.
- **Email** — you can unsubscribe from non-essential emails using the link in those emails; you may still receive transactional messages.
- **SMS** — if you opt in to text alerts, reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.
- **State privacy rights** — depending on where you live (e.g., California and other states), you may have additional rights to access, delete, or opt out of certain processing. To exercise them, contact dryline@gmail.com.

## 10. Children
The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable), and we do not knowingly collect their information.

## 11. Changes
We may update this policy. We'll post the new effective date and, for material changes, provide reasonable notice.
